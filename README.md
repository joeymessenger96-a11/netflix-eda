# Netflix Content Analysis 

Exploratory data analysis of Netflix's full catalogue (8,807 titles) using Python, pandas, and matplotlib.

## Business Questions
1. What is the Movie vs TV Show split?
2. How have content additions changed over time?
3. Which countries produce the most content?
4. What are the most common genres?
5. How have content ratings evolved?

## Key Findings
- **Library composition:** 69.7% Movies, 30.3% TV Shows
- **Growth:** Content additions peaked in 2019 (2,016 titles), then declined
- **Geography:** US dominates (2,809 titles), followed by India (972)
- **Genres:** International Movies, Dramas, and Comedies lead
- **Ratings:** TV MA (mature) is most common and grew fastest
